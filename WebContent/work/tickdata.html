01/2012 - 08/2013

A great company, if really really small, located in Great Falls, VA. I had a lot fun, learned a bunch and got a lot accomplished while working at Tick Data, Inc.. The 'Senior Leadership' at Tick Data, Inc. really care about the people that work for them.

At Deloitte I was able to work with a new IAM tool, IBM Tivoli Identity Manager. It was useful to see how another tool allowed for IAM configuration. I also worked with WebFOCUS, a tool used to create reports and pull data sets from different systems.
As I completed my tasks I was able to see first hand how important communication and networking is. Clearer definitions at different points during our development cycle would have greatly reduced headaches and the number of 'late' nights. The personal relationships I had developed over that time however helped to mitigate further disaster however as people were willing to help out the project I worked on when they might have otherwise found more pressing work to do.
